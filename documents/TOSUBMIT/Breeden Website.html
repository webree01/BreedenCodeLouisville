Carl is my next son, I don't even know what to say about this kid. He's messed up in the head and is in dire need of a haircut. Onto my daughter, Rapunzel. She likes pink and purple, long hair, and is extremely gullible. My youngest is Anakin. He is the sweetest boy ever and I foresee great things in his future.
